Our Story

FastDrive was founded by three passionate friends — Rajveer, Dev, and Krishanpal — with a dream to make car rentals simple, fast, and affordable. Coming from small towns with big ambitions, they combined their technical skills and love for travel to create a platform that puts customers first.

What started as a local car service has now grown into a trusted name across India. With a fleet of reliable cars and a commitment to excellent service, FastDrive ensures that every journey is smooth and memorable.

Join us and experience the freedom of driving on your own terms.
